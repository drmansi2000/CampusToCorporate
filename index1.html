F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I see how many meetings I have attended?

    Yes, in the meeting history section you can see all the meetings you have attended along with their meeting code.

  • Can the interviewer make changes in the text editor section?

    Yes, the interviewer and candidate both can make changes in the text editor section simultaneously.

  • What does C2C mean?

    C2C stands for Campus to corporate. We provide the interviewer as well as the candidates a user-friendly environment with some unique features.

  • Can we change the problem statement?

    The problem statement is only editable by the interviewer! Not by the candidates :-)
